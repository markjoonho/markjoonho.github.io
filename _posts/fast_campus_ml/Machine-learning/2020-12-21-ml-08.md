# Continuous Probability Distribution

## Exponential Distribution

- probability distribution of the time between events
- \\(f(x) = \frac{1}{\mu}e^{-\frac{1}{\mu}x}, x \geq 0\\)

## Normal Distribution (Gaussian)

- type of continuous probability distribution for a real-valued random variable
- \\(P(x) = \frac{1}{\sigma \sqrt{2 \pi}}e^{-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}}\\)

## Standard Normal Distribution

- Normal Distribution where mean = 0 and variance = 1
